About AgriSmart

Key Features

  • Crop recommendations based on soil nutrients and weather conditions
  • Localized suggestions for fertilizer and pesticide usage
  • Water usage insights and guidance for better irrigation planning
  • A trained local chatbot for instant farming assistance
  • Engaging blog and knowledgebase for practical agricultural tips
  • Interactive quizzes and games for learning and engagement
  • Access to historical predictions and crop trends

Technologies Used

  • Backend: Python, Flask, MongoDB
  • Machine Learning: Scikit-learn for predictive modeling
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • APIs: OpenWeatherMap for weather data
  • Deep Learning: A Simple CNN for soil Detection and Analysis

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable, efficient, and tech-driven farming practices. We aim to bridge the gap between traditional agriculture and smart farming by equipping users with data-driven insights and tools that make farming more productive and informed.
