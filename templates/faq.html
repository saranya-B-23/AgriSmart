üå± Crop Prediction

How does the crop prediction feature work?

Our system uses a machine learning model that analyzes soil nutrients, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall to recommend the most suitable crops.

Can I view my past predictions?

Yes, navigate to the 'Prediction History' section after logging in to view your previous predictions.

How do I provide soil data like N, P, K, and pH?

In AgriSmart, you don't need to manually enter soil parameters. You simply upload an image of your soil, and our system uses simple CNN model to detect and autofill the Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), and pH values automatically. Make sure the soil image is clear and follows the User Guidelines for best results.

How accurate is the weather data?

We fetch real-time weather data from reliable APIs. In cases where rainfall data is unavailable or zero, we simulate realistic values to ensure comprehensive predictions.

Why is rainfall data sometimes simulated?

To maintain prediction accuracy, we simulate rainfall data when real-time data is missing or zero, ensuring the model has complete input parameters.
