📌 AgriSmart – User Guidelines

Welcome! This guide explains how to provide accurate inputs for AgriSmart’s intelligent crop, fertilizer, pesticide, and water recommendations.

🖼️ Upload Soil Image

AgriSmart automatically detects your soil type using AI image classification. From this, it estimates:

  • Nitrogen (N)
  • Phosphorus (P)
  • Potassium (K)
  • Soil pH

You do not need to enter these values manually.

Upload a clear photo of bare soil (avoid shadows, grass, stones, or blurry images).

🏙️ City Name (For Weather Data)

The city name is used to fetch live weather data (temperature, humidity, rainfall) using the OpenWeather API.

  • Use full, correct spellings (e.g., Coimbatore, Ahmedabad)
  • Avoid nicknames or short forms (e.g., cmbtr, bglr)
  • For states or districts, use the capital or nearby major city
  • Use OpenWeather Find to check spelling

🌦️ Weather Parameters (Auto-Filled)

Once a valid city is entered, AgriSmart will automatically fetch:

  • 🌡️ Temperature (°C)
  • 💧 Humidity (%)
  • 🌧️ Rainfall (mm)

If the API fails (due to network or spelling error), you will see: ❌ Error fetching weather data: city not found

Try again with proper spelling or choose a nearby major city.
